# Story Telling

## Why Visual Storytelling works
It's not just seeing > it's feeling seen.\
That's when the visual stops being mine and starts belonging to the room.​

It's not just creativity > it's visual inclusion.\
Making ideas accessible to everyone in the room, regardless of how they process information.

## From complexity to clarity
Shared spaces and shared stories are how we navigate this time.\
There are so many ideas worth capturing and perspectives worth understanding.\
But it can be hard to hold them all at once.​

Pulling all the threads together in a beautiful and meaningful visual summary that captures the essence of what matters.\
A time capsule of the moment, to be shared and reflected upon long after people leave the room.​\
This is for the people who care about the room and who believe every voice in it matters.
