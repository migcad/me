# State
State is NOT merely emotion. It's the sum of "all internal systems active and synchronized" inside a human being at a given moment.

Taxonomy #1

## 1. Physiological Activation & Somatic Resourcefulness
- posture
- breathing patterns
- muscular tension
- movement intensity
- hormonal activation
- nervous system regulation
- sleep and recovery
- metabolic energy
- physical vitality

## 2. Emotional & Affective Quality Of Experience
- emotional tone
- mood stability
- emotional resilience
- emotional intensity
- emotional flexibility
- emotional regulation
- capacity for joy/passion/love/courage

## 3. Cognitive Focus & Attentional Filtering Patterns
- attentional focus
- threat vs opportunity orientation
- solution orientation
- future orientation
- pattern recognition
- cognitive framing
- interpretive bias

## 4. Identity Congruence & Self-Concept Embodiment
- self-image
- standards
- identity boundaries
- personal mythology
- role embodiment
- self-worth
- self-trust

## 5. Energetic Momentum & Life-Force Intensity
- drive intensity
- motivational force
- momentum
- urgency
- emotional energy
- charisma
- action readiness

## 6. Linguistic & Internal Dialogue Conditioning
- internal dialogue
- emotional labeling
- semantic framing
- self-talk
- verbal identity reinforcement
- meaning language
- emotional vocabulary
