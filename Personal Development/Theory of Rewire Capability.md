# Rewire Capability

## Capability Scope
```none
Your STATE determines the STORY you tell yourself.
Your STORY determines the STRATEGY you choose.
Your STRATEGY determines your RESULTS.

Change your STATE first.
Because a POWERFUL MIND trapped in a WEAK STATE will still LOSE.

It's a “psychology first” philosophy
```

## Capability Goal
### One word
```none
Useful
```
### One paragraph
```none
Develop the psychological, physiological, emotional, and cognitive capacity
to remain resourceful enough to accurately understand reality and take effective constructive action
under both normal and high-pressure conditions.
```
Emotional = experience

Psychological = interpretation of experience

#### Emotional
The felt experience of internal states.

Examples:
- fear
- anger
- shame
- joy
- calm
- excitement

It answers:
- “What am I feeling right now?”

#### Psychological
The broader mental system that generates interpretation, meaning, beliefs, identity, and thought patterns.

Examples:
- self-beliefs (“I can/can’t handle this”)
- interpretations (“this is dangerous vs manageable”)
- thought loops (rumination, optimism, catastrophizing)
- identity narratives (“I am the kind of person who…”)

It answers:
- “How am I making sense of what I feel and what is happening?”
