# Currency Stengths
Countries tend to downgrade in tier level with time due to debasement.
Countries like UAE and Singapore have been able to upgrade in tier level by implementing low taxation regulation, free-trade policies, rule of law, security, liberal culture, ambition and vision

## Tier 1 $USD
- US has the world reserve currency
- Steady 7% debasement over the last 100 years against scarce desirable assets
- Some years debasement accelerates some it decelerates
- US has a great part of the economy that is free (functioning economy), so it can create extreme wealth (Nvidia, digital assets, digital comms, digital media, self-driving cars, robots, AI)

## Tier 2 currencies cuasi-pegged to the $USD
- pound, euro, yen (China), dram (Armenia), Swiss frank
- These debase a bit faster than the $USD
- Countries don't have the ability to create more of their own currency, so they get into currrency/debt crisis
- Countries looks stable politicaly but suffer from economic stagnation
- Mostly Western world

## Tier 3 currencies loosing around 10% to the $USD per year
- experience around 14% debasement per year
- countires are fighting to keep their head above water

## Tier 4 currencies
- currencies are collapsing against the dollar
- countries are just waiting for international aid
