# Arbitrage Opportunities

## Be cognitive augmented in a world of cognitive reduced people
The bar has never been so low, people now expect:
- highest value possible (100% satisfaction guarantee, unlimited choice)
- whenever-wherever immediacy (one-click, on-demand, same-day delivery, open 24/7/365)
- lowest effort possible (low reasoning: others suggest to me / decide for me)
- lowest sacrifice possible (no internal change, no tradeoffs)
- highest outcome certainty possible (zero patience, zero resilience requiring immediate soothening [food, alcohol, smoking, entertainment, anxiolytics, drugs])

### Derivative #1
Be a long-term visionary & builder in a world of short-term (zero-effort instant-gratification) people
