## Urban Living Index
The challenge for Sydney’s future is to ensure that it responds to population growth yet maintains its world-beating lifestyle and that its liveability rises to match its increasing density.

The population of Sydney is projected to reach 8 million in 2055, increasing by 3.1 million over the next 40 years. As Sydney’s population grows, it also increases in densification.
