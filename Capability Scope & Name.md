# Capability Scope & Name

## Capability Scope
```none
Your STATE determines the STORY you tell yourself.
Your STORY determines the STRATEGY you choose.
Your STRATEGY determines your RESULTS.

Change your STATE first.
Because a POWERFUL MIND trapped in a WEAK STATE will still LOSE.

It's a “psychology first” philosophy
```

## Capability Name
### Question-Based
```none
Can a person regulate their internal state,
reframe the meaning of their story,
and execute effective strategic action
to change their future (overcome a challenge / produce a different life outcome) ?
```
### Noun-Based
```none
State–Story–Strategy Transformation Loop
```
### Verb + Noun
```none
Regulate internal state → Reframe meaning of story → Execute strategic action
```
